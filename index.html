About me

I studied for a degree in Social Working before moving to London, and recently decided to learn to code. I have completed Le Wagon’s Full Stack Developer bootcamp where they not only taught us how to code, but also various workplace and development principles such as Agile Development, growth mindset, OOP and how to work in a small, dedicated team. Since the bootcamp, I’ve been learning to increase my skillset, doing courses on JavaScript, Python, Django, web design, and I’m planning to complete courses I have on Node.js and React.

I enjoy the creative aspects of development, as well as building projects end to end. I am looking forward to continue learning in an exciting and friendly environment.
